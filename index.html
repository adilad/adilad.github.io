Some History

At 8, got my first bike and have been pedaling through life ever since (many bikes later).

At 10, got introduced to my first Pentium 4 computer and that's when I realized what obsession was.

At 13, fell into Sherlock Holmes and became hooked on detective novels and later biographies.

At 14, listened to Bryan Adams and he is still one of my favorites.

2010: Picked up my first acoustic guitar. Country and Folk are still my favorite genres.

2014: Discovered early morning strength training was the key ingredient for a productive day.

2020: Entered a true crime phase and listened to all episodes of Crime Junkie until Aug 2020.

2021: Finished every GTA game in the franchise (still waiting for GTA 6).

2025: Reawakened my lost boxing skills (the gloves are officially back on).
